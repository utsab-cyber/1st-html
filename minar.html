Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is a famous historical monument located in Delhi, India. It is the tallest brick minaret in the world.

Construction of Qutub Minar was started by Qutb-ud-din Aibak in 1193 and later completed by his successors.

The tower is about 73 meters tall and is made of red sandstone and marble. It has beautiful carvings and inscriptions.

Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is one of the most visited tourist attractions in India.
