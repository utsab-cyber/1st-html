Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world, located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Construction of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 and took around 20 years to complete. It is made of white marble and is known for its beautiful architecture and symmetry.

The Taj Mahal is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Millions of tourists visit it every year.

It stands as a symbol of love and is one of the greatest examples of Mughal architecture.
